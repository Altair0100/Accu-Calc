A calculator with a lot of features such as stq, q, rtq, dtq, mtq and much more.
It also contains basic operators i.e. Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication and Division.

Expecting these features in next update:
-Exponent
-Lcm of two numbers
-Even and Odd numbers checker
